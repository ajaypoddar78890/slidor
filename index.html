An iterative methodology for increasingly professional presentations

Brief and graphic universe
We make sure we have a good understanding of the context with designers and project managers, and exchange on your creative ideas and our know-how. We define the level of graphic freedom.
Art direction on PowerPoint
We propose an artistic direction defining the main concepts of the graphic universe that we would like to apply to the whole presentation, in accordance with your brand image.
Declination of the presentation
We send you an advanced version of the presentation and we continue the layout until the end of the project in iterations to obtain a professional rendering to your liking.
Finalization and delivery
We fine-tune the presentation and have it proofread for quality control. We share all the resources we used to create your presentation with you, so you can use them again and again.

Presentations delivered on time

Frequently asked questions
How do you work?
We estimate the time needed to complete your project based on the content of your presentation and our experience with similar projects.
What kind of clients do you work with?
We have experience with clients of all sizes in all industries. We have 1,000 clients from start-ups to large international accounts.
Can you work on very large projects?
We are used to large-scale events: our presentation design agency has 25 designers on permanent contracts who are used to working together through standardized processes to cover needs of all sizes.
How long does a project last?
Our projects usually take between 2 and 4 weeks but can also be done on short notice. We recommend 2 weeks if it is the first time we work together, depending on your needs. We can estimate the time needed for your project very quickly.
What happens after the presentation is over?
We deliver your presentation and everything that was used to build it: you are free to reuse or modify it as you wish and to take advantage of the graphic resources created for any project (Web, Print, Advertising...).
Can you deliver a presentation in 24/48 hours?
We are used to delivering presentations in a hurry, but we allow less time for creative research in this case. If we have been working together for a while, we are able to reduce our working time considerably.
What are your skills beyond PowerPoint?
We bring a wide range of expertise to the presentation: illustration, motion design, photo editing, visual identity, interface creation, front-end, etc. We recruit experienced talent from other areas of design before they become PowerPoint experts. Everything we produce is created in-house: we never work with freelancers. If your need goes beyond our expertise, we will refer you to our partners.
Do you work on the content?
We provide recommendations on how to organize your content to improve the impact of your presentation in the first instance. If your project requires copywriting and/or storytelling, we work with a company whose core business has been in this field for 25 years.
Do you work on other presentation softwares?
We're also experts on Keynote, Pitch and Google Slides. Rendering levels will be less impressive due to the tool's limitations.
Who will I be dealing with?
The agency's founders, project managers, sales staff and designers are in direct contact with our clients to ensure that each project is well communicated.
Do you work in white label?
No, our work processes mean that we are in constant contact with our clients in order to deliver the most beautiful presentations on the market.
How can I be sure that the information I provide is completely confidential?
Our production is fully in-house and secure. We are also used to signing non-disclosure agreements on request.

Customer experience at the heart of presentation creation

At Slidor, we put all our energy into serving our clients in order to meet their expectations perfectly. To do this, we make sure we develop a full understanding of the needs and challenges of each project and accompany you throughout the project. The production process begins with a telephone conversation to fully understand your challenges and to immerse ourselves in your brand universe while understanding your graphic and technical requirements. Following this brief, the designer in charge of your project develops as soon as possible a graphic axis on 4 to 6 slides so that you can visualize the universe we have in mind for your presentation. Once the artistic direction has been validated, it will be applied to all your slides. We regularly send you the reworked slides and take your feedback throughout the iterative production process. This corrective back and forth allows us to deliver a presentation that meets your expectations. This final presentation is designed to be easily modifiable by your teams afterwards, and you can use all the elements of the presentation (illustrations, photos, etc.) as you wish.

Our presentation design agency is also able to offer you a control room. One or more designers will come to your company to work directly under your supervision, which facilitates the exchange of information and eliminates delays between possible corrective visits. Once the service is completed, we propose a final exchange so that you can give us your opinion on the work done, to allow our presentation design agency to always do better and to offer services that are more in line with the requirements of the customers.
